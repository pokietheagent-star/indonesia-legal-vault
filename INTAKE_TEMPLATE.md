## 4. Role routing
### Researcher
Use when:
- statute text is needed,
- regulation status must be verified,
- precedent must be sourced,
- or a legal proposition needs primary-source grounding.

### Strategist
Use when:
- forum choice matters,
- deadlines matter,
- litigation path matters,
- or there is a choice between litigation and non-litigation resolution.

### Clerk
Use when:
- the legal position is already established,
- a memo, contract, letter, pleading, or checklist needs drafting,
- or formatting/redlining is needed.

### Hakim
Use when:
- source-grounded inputs are available,
- conflicting positions need synthesis,
- or a final legal position is required.
