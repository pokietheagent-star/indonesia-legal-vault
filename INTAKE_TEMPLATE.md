# INTAKE_TEMPLATE

Purpose: convert a legal request into a strategy-ready matter file. This template is designed to force early identification of leverage, constraints, deadlines, counterparty behavior, and the practical definition of success before any substantive research or drafting begins.

Use this as the reference intake layer for Hakim and the wider legal OS.
