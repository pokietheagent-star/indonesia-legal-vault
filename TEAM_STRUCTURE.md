# TEAM_STRUCTURE

This repository is organized as a four-role multi-agent legal workflow for Indonesia legal research and drafting.

## Hakim — Lead / Synthesis
- Owns final legal analysis and response quality.
- Integrates research, precedent, drafting, and procedural strategy into one coherent position.
- Resolves conflicts between sources and sets the final analytical frame.

## Researcher — Pasal-id / Precedent Sourcing
- Responsible for locating statutes, regulations, and authoritative legal text.
- Responsible for locating relevant precedents and supporting source material.
- Must use the pasal-id MCP exclusively for all statute and regulation lookups before any legal analysis is drafted or finalized.
- Must not rely on memory, alternate lookup tools, or secondary summaries for statutory/regulatory retrieval.

## Clerk — Drafting / Redlining
- Converts approved analysis into clean drafts, checklists, tables, and edit-ready language.
- Applies formatting discipline, citation hygiene, and consistency checks.
- Redlines and normalizes output without changing substantive legal conclusions.

## Strategist — Procedural / Litigation Path
- Maps the procedural path, forum selection, filing sequence, deadlines, and next steps.
- Flags jurisdiction, venue, timing, burden-shifting, and enforcement considerations.
- Suggests litigation and non-litigation pathways based on the factual posture.

## Operating Rule
- Hakim may synthesize only after the Researcher has sourced the governing law through pasal-id MCP.
- If statutory or regulatory authority is needed, pasal-id MCP is the mandatory source before analysis proceeds.
